Industries
How to use this: pick an industry, pick a level, then rate yourself honestly on each required skill. The ledger will tag exactly which skills to prioritise — and how.

Built for students of the Department of Commerce, Model Degree College Malkangiri, and the wider @DPKlass learning community. Skill benchmarks are synthesised from published CEO/CFO and employer-skills surveys (Deloitte, PwC, Robert Half, NACE) and India-specific employability data (India Skills Report 2026), interpreted for Indian commerce, finance, and business graduates. Treat every self-rating as a private, honest starting point — not a score to show anyone.
