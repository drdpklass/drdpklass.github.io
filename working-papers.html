🌿 ESG, BRSR & Sustainability Research
#1
BRSR Disclosure Quality and the Cost of Equity Capital: Evidence from Indian Listed Firms
Pahi, D. — Revised Draft, April 2026
Exploits India's staggered SEBI BRSR mandate as a natural experiment. Finds a one-SD increase in BRSR quality reduces cost of equity by ~71 bps. Mechanism tests via bid-ask spreads and analyst forecast dispersion confirm information asymmetry channel. Target: top-tier Finance / Accounting journal.

#3
Words That Mislead: Sustainability Rhetoric, Greenwashing Signals, and the Real Cost of Capital in Indian Listed Firms
Pahi, D. — Target: Accounting, Organizations & Society | JAE | BAR
Uses ClimateBERT, FinBERT, and BERTopic on 1,847 BRSR filings (2021–2024) to construct a Greenwashing Rhetoric Index, TCFD Quality Score, and CEO ESG Salience Index. High greenwashing rhetoric is associated with a 47 bps higher cost of equity. Mandatory BRSR reduced vague language but not substantive disclosure quality.

📈 Finance & Economics Research
#7
Cash as Transition Insurance: Climate Policy Exposure, Financing Frictions, and Corporate Cash Holdings
Pahi, D. — Working Paper, April 2026
Exploits staggered cross-country adoption of binding climate-transition regulations (2015–2025) using a Callaway-Sant'Anna DiD estimator on ~500,000 firm-year observations across 62 countries. Documents that a 1-SD increase in transition-risk exposure raises cash-to-assets by ~3.1 pp — an effect concentrated among financially constrained, high-emission firms.
Structural Review Draft Climate Finance Corporate Cash Staggered DiD JEL: G31 G32 Q54 Q58 Download View
#8
Climate Stress, Institutional Governance, and Repayment Fragility in Microfinance: Evidence from a Cross-Country Panel, 2010–2018
Pahi, D. & Mishra, A. P. — Target: Journal of Banking and Finance
Panel OLS with year and country fixed effects on 1,771 MFI-year observations across 205 MFIs in 43 countries. A composite Climate Stress Index (PCA of CO₂, agricultural methane, rural population share) significantly predicts Provisions/NPL ratio. Governance quality buffers climate-to-credit transmission; agricultural exposure amplifies it.
Draft Microfinance Climate Risk Panel Data · Fama-MacBeth JEL: G21 G28 Q54 O16 Download View
