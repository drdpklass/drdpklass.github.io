Income Statement concepts

Revenue

Total income from operations. Top line of P&L.

Revenue = Units × Price

Gross Profit

Revenue minus cost of goods sold.

GP = Revenue − COGS

EBITDA

Earnings before interest, tax, depreciation & amortisation.

EBITDA = EBIT + D&A

EBIT

Operating profit before interest and tax.

EBIT = GP − OpEx − D&A

Net Profit

Final profit after all deductions. Bottom line.

NP = EBIT − Interest − Tax

EPS

Earnings attributable to each equity share.

EPS = NP ÷ Shares
Balance Sheet concepts

Current Assets

Cash, debtors, inventory — convertible within one year.

Fixed Assets

Long-term assets: plant, machinery, property.

Current Liabilities

Short-term obligations due within one year.

Long-term Debt

Borrowings repayable beyond one year.

Shareholders' Equity

Net worth: share capital plus reserves.

Equity = Assets − Liabilities

Working Capital

Funds available for day-to-day operations.

WC = CA − CL
Cash Flow Statement concepts

Operating CF

Cash generated from core business operations.

Investing CF

Cash used for buying/selling assets and investments.

Financing CF

Cash flows from debt, equity issuance, dividends.

Free Cash Flow

Cash available after capital expenditure.

FCF = OCF − CapEx
Ratio categories overview

Liquidity

Short-term payment ability. Current, Quick, Cash, OCF ratios.

Profitability

Profit efficiency. GPM, OPM, NPM, ROE, ROA, ROCE.

Solvency

Long-term stability. D/E, Interest Coverage, CF Coverage.

Activity

Asset usage. Inventory Turnover, Debtor Days, Asset Turnover.

Market / Valuation

Investor metrics. EPS, P/E, Book Value, Dividend Yield.

Cash Flow

CF quality. OCF Ratio, CF-to-Debt, Free Cash Flow yield.
