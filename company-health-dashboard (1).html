Statement of Profit & Loss Schedule III · Part II · Companies Act 2013 ✓ Live data filled
✅ Format as per Schedule III, Part II — Companies Act 2013. Two years: FY2025 (Current) and FY2024 (Previous). Data auto-saved as you type. Green border = live data auto-filled. [auto] fields calculate automatically if left blank.
I & II — Revenue from Operations & Other Income
IV — Expenses [as per Schedule III Part II]
V to XI — Profit / Loss Lines [auto-calculated]
XII — Earnings Per Share [Schedule III Mandatory]
Balance Sheet Schedule III · Part I · Companies Act 2013 ✓ Live data filled
✅ Format as per Schedule III, Part I — Companies Act 2013. Equity & Liabilities on one side, Assets on other. Data auto-saved. [auto] fields calculate automatically.
EQUITY & LIABILITIES — (A) Shareholders' Funds
EQUITY & LIABILITIES — (B) Non-Current Liabilities
EQUITY & LIABILITIES — (C) Current Liabilities & Total Liabilities
ASSETS — (A) Non-Current Assets
ASSETS — (B) Current Assets & Total Assets
Cash Flow Statement AS 3 / Ind AS 7 · Indirect Method · Schedule III ✓ Live data filled
✅ Format as per AS 3 / Ind AS 7 — Indirect Method (as required under Schedule III). Enter cash outflows as negative numbers. [auto] fields calculate automatically.
(A) Cash Flow from Operating Activities — Indirect Method
(B) Cash Flow from Investing Activities
(C) Cash Flow from Financing Activities
(D) Summary — Net Change in Cash & Cash Equivalents
