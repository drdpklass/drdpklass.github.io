This simulation was designed and developed by Dr. Debasis Pahi to help undergraduate commerce and finance students learn financial ratio analysis through hands-on, real-world data entry. Students can input 3 years of any company's financial statements and instantly receive a full health assessment, 16+ calculated ratios, trend charts, and an AI-generated analyst report.
